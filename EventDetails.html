Cyber Security: Safeguarding Against Cyber Attack

About this Event

The Google Developer Student Clubs, in collaboration with the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at CUIET, presents an enriching session titled "Cyber Security: Safeguarding against Cyber Attack" on April 18, 2024, at 1:30 p.m. in Pierre Hall, Chitkara University, Rajpura, Punjab. This session aims to provide practical insights into cybersecurity measures and strategies to mitigate cyber attacks. Our distinguished speaker, Tarun Malhotra, Cyber Security, Compliance Consultant & Auditor, Founder & MD Cyber Cops, will share his expertise and experiences to guide the students towards success in these domains.

NOTE : THIS SESSION IS ONLY FOR CHITKARA UNIVERSITY STUDENTS.
